About Hon. Benson Muema

Biography

Hon. Benson Muema is a progressive leader, seasoned community organizer, and dedicated public servant aspiring to serve the people of Makueni Constituency as their Member of Parliament. Born and raised in Makueni, he is deeply grounded in the lived realities of the constituency and carries a lifelong commitment to advancing the social and economic wellbeing of its people.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in community development, youth empowerment, and public service, Benson has built a reputation as a practical problem-solver and a unifying leader.
